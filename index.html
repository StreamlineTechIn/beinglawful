Being Lawful: Empowered by Knowledge, Guided by Responsibility

As India commemorates the 75th anniversary of its Constitution in 2025, it becomes imperative to engage the younger generation in understanding and embodying the foundational values that have shaped our nation. The "Being Lawful" workshop is designed for students from 8th to 12th standard, with an aim to help them recognize the Constitution's relevance in their daily conduct. This initiative aspires to transform the topic of Constitution from a mere academic subject into a lived experience for students, fostering a generation that upholds and cherishes democratic principles through CBSE school programs on Samvidhan, civics, and government.

Being Lawful Book Overview

In their vulnerability, children need protection from life's injustices. This book serves as a guide, imparting knowledge about their rights, safety measures, and remedies in challenging situations. As they grow, they must understand their responsibilities, respect others' rights, and embrace the significance of 'Being Lawful', the central theme of this book.

As empowered citizens, children have the power to shape India's future. This book inspires them to look beyond self-interest, embracing society, our nation, and the world. The philosophy of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam—the global family—is the vision for a better tomorrow.

May this book offer a fresh perspective, proving invaluable not only to children but also to their parents and teachers.

Author Note

Being Lawful is not just a book about laws - it is about the art of living harmoniously in society.

In writing this book, my purpose was to show that when we choose to be lawful, we contribute to a peaceful and stable society where everyone can grow, learn, and thrive. Lawfulness is not merely about following rules; it is about understanding our place in the larger social fabric and acting with a sense of responsibility and purpose.

Drawing from our Constitution, ancient civilizational texts, and timeless values, Being Lawful invites adolescents and young adults to explore the deeper meaning of citizenship—not just in terms of rights, but equally in terms of duties. A duty-bound society is a strong society. And when citizens become aware, responsible, and active, the nation naturally rises to greater heights.

The workshop and website are designed to carry forward the same mission: to spark curiosity, build awareness, and encourage lawful behavior through real-life scenarios, thoughtful activities, and fun games. Law is not just a subject for lawyers—it’s a tool for every citizen to live with dignity, responsibility, and courage.
