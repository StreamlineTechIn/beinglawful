As India commemorates the 75th anniversary of its Constitution in 2025, it becomes imperative to engage the younger generation in understanding and embodying the foundational values that have shaped our nation. The "Being Lawful" workshop is designed for students from 9th to 12th standard, with an aim to help them recognize the Constitution's relevance in their daily conduct. This initiative aspires to transform the topic of Constitution from a mere academic subject into a lived experience for students, fostering a generation that upholds and cherishes democratic principles.

Being lawful book overview

In their vulnerability, children need protection from life's injustices. This book serves as a guide, imparting knowledge about their rights, safety measures, and remedies in challenging situations. Additionally, as they grow, they must comprehend their responsibilities, respect others' rights, and grasp the significance of 'Being Lawful', the central theme of this book.

As empowered citizens, children have the ability to shape the future of India. The book encourages them to expand their focus beyond self-interest to encompass society, our country, and the entire world.
